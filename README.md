# Hospital Management System: SQL Data Analysis Project
This project contains a comprehensive set of 22 analytical SQL queries designed to extract key performance indicators (KPIs) and business insights from a relational database schema modeling a hospital or clinic management system.
The project demonstrates proficiency in essential SQL concepts including complex joins, aggregation (SUM, AVG, COUNT), ranking (ORDER BY/LIMIT), filtering (WHERE/HAVING), and Common Table Expressions (CTEs).

The primary goal is to provide operational and strategic analysis for hospital administrators by answering critical questions about doctor performance, patient engagement, and financial health.
#### Top 5 Business Questions Answered:
###### Revenue by Doctor (KPI): 
 Total revenue generated by each physician, linking billing to the treatment and the   responsible doctor.(Query 8)
 
 ###### Doctor Performance: 
 Identify the Top 3 doctors based on the total number of unique patients treated.(Query 18)
 
 ###### Financial Health:
 Revenue breakdown by payment status (paid, pending) and total amount collected per payment method.(Queries 5, 17)

###### Patient Engagement: 
Identify patients who have had multiple appointments, signaling high engagement or chronic care needs. (Query 16)

###### Most Common Services:
Determine the average cost and most frequently occurring treatment types. 
(Queries 12, 13)
